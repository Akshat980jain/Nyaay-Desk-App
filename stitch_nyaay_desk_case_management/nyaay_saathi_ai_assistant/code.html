What is a CNR number and why is it important for my case?

smart_toy

A CNR (Case Number Record) is a unique 16-digit ID assigned to every case in the e-Courts system. It is vital because:

  • It allows you to track case status across any court in India.
  • It remains constant even if the case is transferred.
  • It is required for filing subsequent documents.
